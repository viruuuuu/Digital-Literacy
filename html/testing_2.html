Future Updates

We are constantly looking to improve our Budget Buddy application to better serve your needs. Here are some of the future updates that we plan to incorporate:

  • Linking to Your Personal G-Pay Account: Seamlessly connect your G-Pay account to track expenses directly through our platform, making it easier to manage all transactions in one place.
  • Integrating with Your Computer's Calendar for Daily Reminders: Set up daily reminders to log expenses or review your budget, ensuring you never miss a chance to keep your finances on track.
  • Linking with Friends' Budget Buddy Accounts: Collaborate with friends by linking Budget Buddy accounts, allowing you to share expense insights, budget goals, and even group savings plans.
